## COMPLETE MODULE MAP (v2)

---

### MODULE 1 — DASHBOARD
- Role-based views: Admin sees everything; Cashier sees only POS + today's sales
- Live KPIs: Revenue · Profit · Stock value · Receivables · Payables
- Alerts panel: Low stock · Overdue invoices · Expiring batches · Pending orders
- Today vs yesterday vs last week comparison
- Top 5 products by revenue and by quantity
- Quick Actions bar

---

### MODULE 2 — POINT OF SALE (POS)
- Barcode scan + manual product search
- Batch/lot selection on product click
- Cart: per-item discount, qty, unit price override
- Customer attachment → pulls credit limit, loyalty points, outstanding balance
- Credit limit enforcement: warn or block sale if customer over limit
- Promotions auto-applied at cart level (buy 2 get 1, 10% off category, etc.)
- Gift card redemption at checkout
- Loyalty points earn + redeem at checkout
- Payment: cash · card · split · partial · advance deduction
- Hold & resume named carts
- Exchange (return + new sale in one flow)
- Offline mode with sync on reconnect
- Receipt: print thermal / A4 / SMS / WhatsApp
- Day-end register close: cash counted vs system expected, variance recorded

---

### MODULE 3 — QUOTATIONS & ESTIMATES
*Was entirely missing from v1. Standard in every professional wholesale system.*

- Create quotation for retail or wholesale customer
- Add products, qty, prices, discount per line
- Quotation expiry date
- Send to customer via email / WhatsApp / PDF
- Customer accepts → converts to confirmed sale order automatically
- Track quotation status: Draft → Sent → Accepted → Rejected → Expired
- Quotation history per customer

---

### MODULE 4 — SALES & ORDERS
**Retail Sales**
- From POS → auto invoice

**Wholesale Orders**
- Quotation → Order → Invoice → Dispatch → Delivery
- Bulk order form: carton/unit pricing
- Payment terms: Net 30 / 60 / 90 / custom installments / deferred
- Partial delivery + back-order auto-created for remainder
- Order status pipeline: Draft → Confirmed → Packed → Dispatched → Delivered

**Shared**
- Per-sale profit margin visible
- Held orders management
- Sale history with full filters

---

### MODULE 5 — PURCHASES & PROCUREMENT
- Supplier quotation comparison (compare 2–3 suppliers side by side)
- Purchase Order (PO) creation
- Goods Received Note (GRN) against PO
- Purchase invoice matching to GRN (3-way match)
- Purchase return → debit note to supplier
- Landed cost: freight + customs + handling spread across stock cost price
- Automated reorder: stock hits threshold → draft PO generated
- Supplier performance log: on-time rate, return rate

---

### MODULE 6 — DELIVERY & DISPATCH
*Was entirely missing from v1.*

- Create delivery note against confirmed sale order
- Assign delivery to driver / vehicle
- Dispatch status: Pending → Packed → Out for Delivery → Delivered / Failed
- Packing slip generation (what's in the box, per order)
- Proof of delivery (mark delivered + note)
- Failed delivery → reschedule or return to stock
- Delivery history per customer per order

---

### MODULE 7 — INVENTORY
- Live stock levels per product per batch/lot
- Batch tracking: manufacture date, expiry, cost price per batch
- Serial number tracking (electronics, high-value items)
- Barcode label printing: generate + print product labels (thermal/A4)
- Stock adjustment: damage / theft / correction with reason log
- Stock transfer between branches/locations
- Cycle count / stocktake with variance approval workflow
- Dead stock report: zero movement in 30/60/90 days
- Expiry alerts: X days before batch expires
- Demand forecasting: suggest reorder qty based on sales velocity

---

### MODULE 8 — PRODUCTS & CATALOG
- Product card: name, SKU, barcode, category, UOM
- Dual pricing: retail price + wholesale price
- Tiered pricing: qty-based price breaks (10+ units → price A, 50+ → price B)
- Product variants: size, color, weight
- Product images
- Tax/VAT assignment per product
- Reorder point + preferred supplier per product
- Cost price history
- **Kits / Bundles**: group multiple products into one sellable bundle, sold as single SKU, stock deducted from components automatically

---

### MODULE 9 — PROMOTIONS & DISCOUNT ENGINE
*Was missing from v1 as a standalone module.*

- Promotion types:
  - Percentage off product / category / cart total
  - Buy X get Y free
  - Buy X qty → get price B (volume discount)
  - Spend Rs.X → get Rs.Y discount
  - Time-limited flash promotions (start/end datetime)
  - Customer-group-specific promotions (retail-only or wholesale-only)
- Promotions stack rules: which promotions can combine, which can't
- Coupon codes: single-use / multi-use / per-customer limit
- Auto-apply vs manual apply at POS
- Promotion performance report: how many used, revenue impact, margin impact

---

### MODULE 10 — LOYALTY PROGRAM
*Was partially mentioned but not structured in v1.*

- Points earn rules: Rs.X spent = Y points

- Redeem rules: Y points = Rs.Z discount at checkout
- Points expiry policy
- Loyalty balance visible at POS when customer selected
- Points history per customer
- Loyalty report: active members, points liability, redemption rate

## COMPLETE DATA FLOW

```
[Quotation] → accepted → [Sale Order]
[Sale Order] → confirmed → [Delivery Note] + [Invoice]
[Delivery] → dispatched → [Proof of Delivery] → [Invoice marked delivered]
[POS Sale] → [Invoice] → [Inventory reduced] → [Finance updated] → [Loyalty points added]
[Promotion] → auto-evaluated at cart → [Discount applied]
[Gift Card] → redeemed → [Balance deducted]
[Purchase] → GRN received → [Inventory increased] → [Landed cost applied] → [Invoice matched]
[Return] → [Inventory restocked] → [Credit note to customer wallet OR refund]
[Low Stock] → [Alert] → [Draft PO auto-created]
[Attendance] → [Payroll calculated] → [Payslip generated]
[Every transaction] → [Audit log entry]
```
